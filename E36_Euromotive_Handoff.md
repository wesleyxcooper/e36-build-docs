## Scope Discussion at Dropoff

Talk through the following with the tech before leaving the engine:

1. **360° Thrust Bearing Mod** — machine both sides of the #4 main cap to accept the full-circle thrust washers (brought with the engine — see "034-block thrust washers" above). Show them the reference video:  
   📹 [Five (5) Cylinder VW Thrust Washer Repair (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hCIZny5uc4Q)  
   Community confirmation: Carson Heter (Precision 6266 Gen2 07K build) — "I did the 360° thrust on both sides." Budget ~$50–150 machining absorbed into longblock labor.

2. **Align Hone** — required after ARP main stud installation. Do not skip.

3. **Bore Inspection + Hone** — bore to SPA 83mm piston spec. **SPA specifies 0.08mm piston-to-wall clearance** — confirm this number with shop; they may recommend opening it slightly for a drift/high-RPM application.

4. **Deck Flatness Check** — standard during longblock teardown.

5. **Head Pressure Test** — standard; confirm head will hold before assembly.

6. **Valve Seat Cut** — only if running Inconel exhaust valves (bring decision at dropoff). If not, lap existing valves.

7. **Crank Journal Measurement** — mic all 5 main journals and 5 rod journals before ordering King XP bearings. Call/text with measurements before ordering; bring bearings to a follow-up appointment or leave a card for shop to order.

8. **TTRS Oil Squirter Install** — 5× squirters (brought with engine). Thread-in, torque to 27 ft-lbs per VW Erwin spec. Install during short block assembly.
